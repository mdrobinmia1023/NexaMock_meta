♿ Accessibility Guide
1
🌐 Paste Your Facebook URL
Go to the Page Audit Checker section. Find the input box labeled "Facebook Page / Profile URL". Paste your full Facebook link there — for example: https://facebook.com/yourpagename. Then click the ⚡ CHECK button.
💡 Tip: Copy your page URL directly from your browser address bar while visiting your Facebook page.
2
⚡ Instant Report Bar
Below the main checker there's a purple "Instant Facebook Report" bar. Paste any Facebook ID or page link here and click Generate Report. You'll instantly see a success report — no waiting needed.
💡 Tip: This gives a fast overview without running the full AI audit.
3
📊 Reading Your Report
After checking, a full report appears with a score out of 100. Each of the 10 metrics is shown in green (good), yellow (needs work), or red (problem). Hover over any red item to see the exact solution popup.
💡 Tip: Download the report as PDF or JPG to share with your client.
4
🏷 Tag Suggestions
Go to Power Tools → Tag Suggestions. Type your business niche (e.g. "Bakery" or "Fashion"). Choose English, Bengali, or Both. Click Generate Tags. Click any tag chip to copy it instantly.
5
✍️ Bio Generator
Go to Power Tools → Bio Generator. Fill in your business name, type, service, and location. Choose a tone. Click Generate Bio — you'll get 3 ready-to-use bio options. Click Copy Bio to grab it.
6
💚 Client Health Report
Go to Power Tools → Health Report. Paste your client's Facebook URL. Enter their name. Click Generate. Download the professional health report PDF to send to your client.
7
🕐 History & Theme
Click the 🕐 button (bottom-right area) to open your audit history. All past checks are saved. Use the 🌙/☀️ toggle in the header to switch between Dark and Light themes.
8
💬 NEXA AI Helper
Click the 💬 button (bottom-right corner) to open the AI chat. Ask any question about Facebook page optimization. You can write in English or বাংলা — the AI understands both.
